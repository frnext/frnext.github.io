### Quiz  

- How do you say "Good morning" in French?  
  {.quiz-multichoice}  

  - ==Bonsoir==  
  - ==Salut==  
  - **==Bonjour==**  
    {.quiz-correct}  

- Which article would you use with the noun **==maison==** (house)?  
  {.quiz-multichoice}

  - ==les==
